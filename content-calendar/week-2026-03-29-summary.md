### Reel 5 — Actionable Tips: 3 Bodyweight Exercises
**Pillar:** Actionable Tips

**HOOK (first 3 seconds):**
> "Three exercises. No equipment. Your knees, hips, and back at 70 will thank you."

---

**SCRIPT (30–45 seconds — direct to camera):**

> Three exercises. No equipment. Your knees, hips, and back at 70 will thank you.
>
> Number one: the split squat. One foot forward, one foot back. Lower your back knee toward the floor — slowly. This builds the quad and glute strength that keeps your knees tracking correctly for decades. Three sets of eight per side.
>
> Number two: the hip hinge. Stand hip-width apart, soft bend in the knees, push your hips back — not down. Feel your hamstrings load. This is the pattern that protects your lower back and builds posterior chain strength without a single weight. Three sets of ten.
>
> Number three: the dead hang. Find a bar, a beam, even a sturdy door frame. Hang for 30 seconds. This decompresses your spine, builds grip strength — which is strongly linked to longevity — and improves shoulder health over time. Daily if you can.
>
> These aren't trending. They're not flashy. But the research on what keeps people functional into their 70s and 80s keeps pointing at exactly these patterns.
>
> Save this and come back to it.

**END CTA:**
> "Save this — and follow for weekly exercises backed by actual science."
