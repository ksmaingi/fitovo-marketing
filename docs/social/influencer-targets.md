## Outreach operating rules

1. **Never mass-DM.** Every message is personal to the recipient â€” reference a specific post / episode / talk.
2. **Lead with value, not ask.** First message: a thoughtful take + free product. The ask comes in conversation #2 or #3.
3. **One pitch per week.** Batch outreach on Friday afternoons. Don't spam.
4. **Track everything** in this file. Update status after every meaningful touchpoint.
5. **If ignored twice, move on.** One polite follow-up at 7 days. After that, re-engage organically in 3 months, not before.
6. **Budget discipline.** Tier 2 spend is capped at $8k across Year 1. Don't blow it on one creator without data showing it will return.
