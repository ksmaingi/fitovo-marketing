Implementation Roadmap

Three phases from pre-launch through Month 6 post-launch. Weekly tactical review every Monday, full strategic review quarterly.

Phase 1 — Pre-Launch
Apr 13 – May 3
Tease product · paid ads carry waitlist · ship 2 Reels/week consistently
  • W3: Ship 2 Reels + 1 Carousel + 5 Stories
  • W3: Start Meta Lead Gen at $15/day (not $10)
  • W3: Start 10-min daily engagement block (Mon–Fri)
  • W4: Hold paid at $15/day unless CPA < $2 confirmed
  • W5: Launch-eve founder Reel · email waitlist 3 days out
Goals

IG: 12 → 50 · Waitlist: 5 → 250–300 · TikTok: 0 → 15–20

Phase 2 — Launch
May 4 – May 31
Ship · convert waitlist · first testimonials · shift pillar to user stories
  • W1: Launch Reel Monday · email blast · paid burst $30–40/day × 5 days (capped)
  • W2: Back to 2 Reels/week; recruit 1–2 testimonials from waitlist
  • W3: First user spotlight Carousel ships
  • W4: Month-1 founder retrospective Reel + LinkedIn post
  • Shift paid to retargeting + waitlist lookalikes
Goals

IG end-May: 150–200 · Installs W1: 100–120 · Paying-user conversion > 15%

Phase 3 — Scale
Jun – Oct 2026
Hold 2-Reel cadence · #FitovoMember launches · paid ads keep carrying acquisition
  • M2: Launch #FitovoMember hashtag · first monthly Live
  • M3: Add LinkedIn founder series (text-only, low-effort)
  • M4: Launch referral programme · drop to 1 Reel/wk in summer if needed
  • M5: One podcast appearance (realistic mid-size show)
  • M6: 6-month retrospective · quarterly strategy refresh
